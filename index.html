About Us

Seven-Skies, a project by SHREE RAM KRUPA ENTERPRISE, established in 2006, is located in the Sanskari Nagri of Gujarat, Vadodara. Our modern hotel blends heritage with grandeur, offering 22 luxurious rooms with stunning views.

With fine dining, spacious banquets, and top-class amenities, Seven-Skies ensures a mesmerizing stay for leisure or business.
