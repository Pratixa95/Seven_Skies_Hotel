About Us

Seven-Skies project by SHREE RAM KRUPA ENTERPRISE, established on 18 March 2006, is located in the Sanskari Nagri of Gujarat, Vadodara, at Gotri, in close vicinity of the city center, Vadodara Airport, and Railway station. TaraSuns is a Modern hotel which evokes the grandeur and lavishness of the Land of Maharaja Sayajirao. All the 22 rooms are stately and lavishly appointed with exquisite embellishments.

The TaraSuns is a perfect blend of our heritage firmly rooted in traditions and an exclusive contemporary five-star ambience. You can relish the scenic views of the green Vadodara city and the Garden from every room and every Banquet Hall. The luxurious offerings and the immaculate services and facilities will make your holiday indulgent and memorable.

TaraSuns pays extra attention to the safety and well-being of the guests by providing sanitised stays and touchless hospitality that come with the reassurance of Suraksha. With spacious rooms, luxurious amenities, and fine dining options, TaraSuns is all about living life 'King-size'! Whether you are in Vadodara for a weekend stay, a family vacation, or a business trip, a stay at TaraSuns promises a truly mesmerising experience.
